Haley Desilets Designs

Our Promise To You

Haley Desilets Designs promises to help businesses achieve their fullest potential through better branding and messaging with the use of design. We strive to create dynamic, thoughtful and visually stunning graphic design for all of our clients. Our highly detail oriented methodology of design allows for us to fulfil hopes, needs, and dreams for every business.

We believe in working with our clients individually through careful communication and collaboration to ensure the design journey is as seamlessly as possible.

Our Origins

Haley Desilets Designs was founded in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada in 2021 by Haley Desilets when she began her graphic design career as a student of the Graphic Design program at Algonquin College. She will be graduating in 2023 and so far has successfully attained dean's list status in her entire first year of study. Haley spends her time at Algonquin soaking up every piece of knowledge possible. She is a passionate designer and fine artist. When she is not working on graphic design, she can be found painting landscapes, dog portraits, and much more. Her love of graphic design is fueled by spending time with her dog and being active outside in the sun.
